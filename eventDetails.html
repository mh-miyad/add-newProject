Description

Deep Learning is one of the most highly sought after skills in the technology field. If you want to break into Artificial Intelligence, this seminar will help understand the core concepts and applications of AI and Deep Learning.

Innosoft Gulf, the leading Artificial Intelligence and Big Data Education Center in the Middle East and North Africa, is very pleased to invite you, your friends and colleagues to a Free Seminar on Artificial Intelligence: Deep Learning and Computer Vision. This event will be held at our education center in Dubai Knowledge Park (Block 6, Office F02)
